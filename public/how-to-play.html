How to Play

Game Basics

PongBattle.io is a modern take on the classic Pong game. Control your paddle to hit the ball and score points against your opponent.

Controls

  • Mouse Movement: Move your mouse up and down to control your paddle
  • W/S Keys: Alternative controls - W to move up, S to move down
  • Arrow Keys: Up arrow to move up, Down arrow to move down

Game Modes

  • Quick Match: Play against a random opponent online
  • Play with Friend: Create a private room and share the code with a friend
  • Practice vs AI: Play against the computer to improve your skills

Scoring

Score a point when your opponent misses the ball. The first player to reach 10 points wins the match.

Paddle Techniques

The angle at which the ball bounces depends on where it hits your paddle:

  • Hit with the center for a straight return
  • Hit with the top or bottom edges for more extreme angles
  • The ball speeds up slightly with each hit, making the game progressively more challenging
  • The ball's maximum speed is capped after approximately 10 hits

Tips & Strategies

  • Anticipate your opponent's moves by watching their paddle position
  • Use the edges of your paddle to create difficult angles for your opponent
  • Stay near the center when possible to minimize movement distance
  • Practice against the AI to improve your reflexes
